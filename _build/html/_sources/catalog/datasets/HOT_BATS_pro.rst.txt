How to Acknowledge
******************

The data and data description were downloaded from BCO-DMO (https://www.bco-dmo.org/dataset/3381).



This data came from a Center for Microbial Oceanography: Research and Education (C-MORE) project.


This dataset was submitted by the Chisholm Lab at MIT: https://chisholmlab.mit.edu/.



Metadata associated with the Prochlorococcus data at HOT can be downloaded from http://hahana.soest.hawaii.edu/hot/hot-dogs/bextraction.html.



For details on the methods used to collect data on HOT cruises, see http://hahana.soest.hawaii.edu/hot/methods/results.html.


Metadata associated with the Prochlorococcus data at BATS can be downloaded from http://bats.bios.edu/bats-data/.



For details on the methods used to collect data on BATS cruises, see http://bats.bios.edu/about/cruise-information/.
